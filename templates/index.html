    Guide

    This app helps you to visualize the bus service route from the start to the end.

    In some cases, you may want to take a tour around Singapore via public buses. :) Instead of taking tour guided bus, you can now take public buses and know where the bus is heading to. With this, you are mostlikely prefer to explore rather than going to a particular location. Moreover, I believe this is a better way of exploring around Singapore streets. :)

    Finding strategic location for your property. For example, you may want to visualize all buses near your location. This allows you to know what's the coverage of your property if you were to travel by bus.
